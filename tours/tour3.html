Overview

The most elevated experience of space that we offer at the moment.
7-8 people per flight, duration of about 3 days

The ISS tour is the most full and daring tour we offer. After being launched by our flagship rocket, the SpaceX Starship, you will spend about 12 hours of your 3 days in space going up to and down from the orbit of the ISS (about 408km), where you will dock and live onboard for two days. During this time, you will get exclusive access to most of the ISS, where you can learn more about the research humans do in space. After two days, your starship will undock from the ISS, travelling at the same altitude and give you an oppurtunity to take the view of the Earth in. After 12 hours in that orbit, you will return to Earth.
