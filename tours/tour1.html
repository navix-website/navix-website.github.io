Overview

The Around the World Tour™ is our most popular, least expensive and most safest tour.
20-25 people per flight; duration of three hours

The objective of this tour is simple and does not even require a rocket to launch: you will climb onto our Challenger fleet of high-altitude planes to travel around the world. At the apogee (the highest point during the spaceflight), your orbital velocity will be around 28000 km/h, at an altitude of about 90 km above the Earth's surface. You will experience weightlessness for about 10-15 minutes in the middle of flight, and the Earth will revolve once as you ascend to the stars and descend back to home.
