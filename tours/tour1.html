Overview

The Around the World Tour™ is our most popular, least expensive and most safest tour.
12-15 people per flight, duration of three hours

The objective of this tour is simple, and does not even require a rocket to launch: you will climb onto our Challenger fleet of high-altitude planes to travel around the world. At the apogee (the highest point during the spaceflight), your orbital velocity will be around 17000 km/h, at an altitude of about 90km above the Earth's surface. You will experience weightlessness for about 15 minutes in the middle of flight, and revolve the Earth once as you ascend to the stars and descend back to home.

We at Navix require that the customer is completely familiar with the tour's parameters, objectives and neccessary information.
  • Safety is our number one concern, and the quarantine and training aspects of the tour cannot be skipped. In accordance, we also reserve the right to deny access to any person who compromises the safety of the tour, on any criteria as might be deemed appropriate. Said person will be granted a full refund and will be barred from all future Navix spaceflights for the next three years. This decision can be appealed.
  • Please note that this tour involves at least 15 hours of dedicated training and requires 6-7 days of dedicated preparation and quarantine for the tour. You are requested to clear your schedule three weeks prior to the tour. Please do not register if the time aspect of the tour is unfavorable to you. All people who do not complete the prerequisite training and quarantine will not be allowed the tour.
  • Failure to stick to the deadlines and schedule after payment will result in a 50% refund, and the customer will be prohibited from the tour. The chosen tour date cannot be shifted in most circumstances, unless in exceptional cases with proper documentation. If it is such a case, the tour date for the customer will be shifted about 6 months into the future, or the customer will recieve a 75% refund [choice of the customer].
