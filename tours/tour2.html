Overview

This tour helps you reach the pinnacle of human innovation.
13-14 people per flight, duration of about 20 hours

You will be launched to space by the Navix fleet of three SpaceX Dragon rockets (Dragon1, Dragon 3 or Dragon4). The ride up takes about 3 hours, and the ride down about 4 hours. For the approximately 13 hours of spaceflight in orbit, you will be hurtling through space at a speed of 28,500 km/h around the earth, revolving approximately every 90 minutes at a height of about 300km above sea level. You will experience weightlessness for most of the time in orbit. For the time you are in orbit, you will live like an astronaut, with many special activities available that will cater to your personal interest

All people who do not complete the prerequisite training and quarantine will not be allowed on the tour. We also reserve the right to deny access to any person who compromises the safety of the tour, on any criteria as might be deemed appropriate.

  • Failure to stick to the deadlines and schedule after payment will result in a 25% refund, and the customer will be prohibited from the tour. The chosen tour date cannot be shifted in most circumstances. If you miss the tour while satisfying prerequisite conditions, you will recieve no refund.
