Cancellation and Refunds

Listed below are the cases of cancellation and the applicable refunds, other relevant reparations and legal consequences for each specific case:

  • Case 1: You registered for the tour, but did not complete the payment within a week.
    In such a case, your booking is considered to be cancelled, and you will be barred from registration for the next 3 months.
  • Case 2: You registered for the tour, paid the requisite amount, and wish to gain a refund.
    In such a case, you will have to reach out to our customer support team. As long as the ticket is raised within 24 hours, you will recieve a full refund. If the ticket is raised after 24 hours of payment, you will be refunded a specific portion of your payment, as given below:
    • For the Around the World™ Tour, you will be refunded 50% of your payment.
    • For the Pinnacle™ Tour, you will be refunded 65% of your payment.
    • For the ISS™ Tour, you will be refunded 80% of your payment.
  • Case 3: You registered for the tour, paid the requisite amount, and wish to gain a refund because of uncontrollable external circumstances*.
    In such a case, you will have to reach out to our customer support team and provide them with proper documentation. After review and if you are found to be applicable, you will be asked to choose between a tour date shift into the future based on avaibility and being refunded a specific portion of your payment in a one-time payment, as given below:
    • For the Around the World™ Tour, you will be refunded 75% of your payment.
    • For the Pinnacle™ Tour, you will be refunded 90% of your payment.
    • For the ISS™ Tour, you will be refunded 90% of your payment.
  • Case 4: You registered and paid for the tour, but failed to satisfy prerequisite conditions.
    In such a case, you will be refunded 25% of your tour payment and be barred from registration for the next 12 months.
  • Case 5: You registered and paid for the tour, but did not participate in the tour.
    Unless you could not attend the tour because of uncontrollable external circumstances*, you will not be refunded any portion of your payment.
  • Case 6: You registered and paid for the tour, but are found ineligible for participation.
    You will be given a full refund and be barred from registration for the next 3 years.
*Uncontrollable external circumstances are circumstances in which you can conclusively and utterly prove you had no control over and also which affect your state of being profoundly (ex: death of a family member).
